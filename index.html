What do I need to rent a trailer?+
We keep things straightforward, but there are a few important expectations. You'll need a valid driver's license, proof of insurance, and a properly equipped tow vehicle. A credit card is required for your security deposit at the time of booking, and payment of the rental fee is due at pick-up. If you have questions about tow-vehicle compatability, please contact us. We'll verify your trailer hitch setup matches the trailer class at pickup.
How do I book a trailer?+
Head to ironhawkrentals.com to browse our trailers, check live availability, and complete your booking online. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details you need for pickup.
Is same-day pickup available?+
Yes — when trailers are available, same-day pickup is often possible. Booking online is the fastest way to check and reserve. For last-minute needs, feel free to contact us directly.
When do I pay for the rental?+
A deposit is required at booking to reserve your dates. The full rental payment is due at pickup. After the trailer is returned on time and in good condition, the security deposit portion will be refunded to you.
What can I expect at pick-up?+
At pick-up, we'll walk through a quick check-in - review and sign the trailer rental agreement, complete an inspection and safety check, verify your license and insurance, and get you hooked up and ready to haul.
What can I expect at return?+
Please return the trailer at your scheduled time so we can check it in. We're typically on-site for returns, but early drop-offs are welcome - just let us know ahead of time. A secure key drop is also available if needed.
What if I need to extend my rental?+
Just get in touch before your scheduled return time and we'll do our best to accommodate you. Extensions are subject to availability and priced at our standard daily rate.
Do you offer delivery?+
We do not offer delivery of trailers at this time. All reservations require pick-up from our West Olathe location.
What is your cancellation policy?+
Plans change — we get it. Please review the cancellation policy on your booking confirmation or contact us directly and we'll work with you. Cancellations within 24-hours of the reservation, or failure to arrive for pick-up at the scheduled time, may result in forfeiture of the security deposit.
